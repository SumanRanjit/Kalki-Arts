About Kalki Studio

A Decade of Creative Spirit

It has been 10 incredible years — one whole decade — of artistic journey at Kalki Studio. Throughout this time, we've worked towards creating a vibrant and creative destination for art. We've been honoured to collaborate with friends from across Nepal and various foreign countries, coming together through colours, pencils, brushes, and artistic tools to share and observe art.

Our work has always been deeply rooted in the context of our nation's socio-cultural landscape. We've explored and reflected on our lifestyles through light, shade, and colour, resulting in powerful creative expressions. Kalki Studio has become a gathering place for artists from diverse communities who are passionate about art in all its forms.

Over the past 8 years, students from Srijana College of Fine Arts have joined hands with us. We've also welcomed international artists and several organisations have hosted workshops here. Kalki Studio is not just a workspace — it's a sanctuary for artists. Visitors often leave feeling refreshed and inspired.

We are deeply engaged in themes like the Himalayan region, climate change, wildlife, and nature conservation. We are proud to offer hand-poked traditional tattoos — one of the world's oldest forms of body art. Located at Thamel Satghumti, near Paknajol Chowk, Kathmandu.
